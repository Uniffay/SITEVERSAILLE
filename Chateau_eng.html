Presentation of the Castle

Listed since 1979 as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Palace of Versailles was built by Louis XIII and later enlarged by his son Louis XIV.
The kings of France succeeded one another, each in turn embellishing the castle until 1789 during the French Revolution.
The castle of Versailles has more than 2300 rooms and covers 63 154m², the domain of the castle of Versailles covers more than 800 hectares,
this one is composed of the castle as well as the Gardens, the Park, the domain of Trianon and the Stables.
